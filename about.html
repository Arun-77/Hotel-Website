About Us

Welcome to Hotel Royal, your number one tourist hotel in the city has always dedicated to giving you the very best of management & services, with a focus on providing clean rooms, Hygienic foods & first-class Room services to our Customers.

Founded in 1958, Hotel Royal has come a long way from its beginnings. When Our Father first started out, his passion for providing a stay for travellers & visitors made to quit day job, so that Hotel Royal can offer you one of the world's best service. We now serve Customers from all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
